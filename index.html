Hello, welcome to my website! I am currently an Assistant Professor at the Business School of Renmin University of China. I obtained my PhD degree at Pennsylvania State University in 2020. My research fields are industrial organization and macro development. In particular, I am interested in topics on productivity and innovation, both from the micro and macro perspectives. In my research, I seek to combine accurate micro estimates with meaningful macro and managerial implications.
