Business School, Renmin University of China

Email: chenzhiyuan@rmbs.ruc.edu.cn

Hello, welcome to my website! I obtained my PhD degree at the Department of Economics, Pennsylvania State University. My research fields are industrial organization and macro development. In particular, I am interested in topics on productivity and innovation, both from the micro and macro perspectives. In my research, I seek to combine accurate micro estimates with meaningful policy and managerial implications, with an emphasis on the modelling and methodology development.
